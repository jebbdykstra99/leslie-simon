About Leslie

Every Exceptional Hire Shapes the Culture That Defines Your Organization.

For more than 25 years, Leslie has partnered with organizations navigating growth, transformation, and innovation. Her experience spans merchandising, product leadership, global licensing and brand partnerships, executive search, and talent strategy across consumer brands, financial services, professional sports, healthcare and medical devices, technology, energy and sustainability, and emerging industries.

Working alongside executives and founders, Leslie helps organizations identify transformative talent, strengthen leadership, and build high-performing teams that create lasting business value.

In addition to executive search, Leslie has built and scaled nationally recognized university recruiting, internship programs, and early-career development initiatives, creating sustainable talent pipelines that strengthen organizations for long-term success.

Her approach combines strategic thinking, trusted partnerships, and modern talent strategies to help organizations navigate change and build teams positioned for the future.
