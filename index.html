The Person Behind the Firm

25 years. One conviction:
find the right person.

Leslie Simon's career runs from the sales floor to the boardroom — a 25-year through-line that gives her a recruiter's empathy and a brand executive's discernment. She began at Randa Accessories in 1997 as a Senior Field Retail Merchandiser (earning Rookie of the Year, then Field Merchandiser of the Year), and rose through progressive leadership roles to Senior Product Merchandiser, developing $MM account strategies for Ben Sherman, Kohl's Croft & Barrow, and other leading brands.

Her creative chapter as Creative Director of Product at The Tie Bar (2012–2015) doubled annual sales in under two years, launched the "EDIT Program" exclusively for Nordstrom, and produced the Dwyane Wade celebrity accessories line. In 2015, she founded Leslie Simon Recruiting — serving all industries, with deep roots in fashion and consumer brands. Since 2018, she has concurrently served as Director of Talent & Engagement at Randa Apparel & Accessories, a global powerhouse with 30+ brands.
